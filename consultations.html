Natal Attunement

1 hour | P2000

Book a Session

The natal reading explores the basis of your birth chart: a celestial depiction of the solar system on the time of your birth. For 60 Minutes, we talk about what patterns are there in your life, which hardships may arise, which strengths are present. Know that in any reading, its a two way street, where the reading is enriched by the stories you bring mapped into the stories woven in the sky.

Transits and Timing

1 hour | P2000

Book a Session

Transits reveal the forecast of the present, what the sky is doing now and how it speaks to your life. When to brace for storms, when to rest, when to act. Preparation and planning are the focus here: for an hour, we talk about your concerns and how the outcomes may unfold for the period you want to look at. Bring a question, a timeframe, or just a sense of what's weighing on you, and we discuss what is in store for these times.
